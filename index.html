Breakfast & practice facilities

Lunch & Guinness in the clubhouse

Afternoon

⛳ Tralee Golf Club
Barrow, Co. Kerry · Rating: 4.8/5 (733 reviews)
📞 +353 66 713 6379
Designed by Arnold Palmer · Dramatic sea views on every hole · Described as "world class" by reviewers
Morning
Tralee Golf Club — Arnold Palmer design
Consistently rated one of Ireland's finest links, with dramatic clifftop holes and sweeping Atlantic views. Palmer said it was the most beautiful piece of land he had ever seen for a golf course. A caddie is a must here.
Post-round
Breakfast sandwich & lunch at the clubhouse
Reviewers love the breakfast sandwich before the round and the restaurant's views. Highly rated food and service.
Afternoon
Drive to Old Head / Kinsale area (~2h 30m)

Evening
Dinner in Kinsale
Kinsale is the gourmet capital of Ireland — Fishy Fishy and The Bulman are outstanding. Well worth the drive.

⛳ Old Head Golf Links
Kinsale, Co. Cork · Rating: 4.6/5 (756 reviews)
📞 +353 21 477 8444 · Opens 8:30 AM
Built on a 220-acre headland jutting into the Atlantic · 7 km of cliff edge · One of the most visually dramatic courses in the world · No public access to the headland — golf only
Morning
Old Head Golf Links — bucket list course
Perched on a dramatic headland 300 feet above the Atlantic. 17 of 18 holes have ocean views. Reviewers call it "carved from the edge of the world." One of the most expensive — and most unforgettable — rounds in Irish golf.
Post-round
Lighthouse restaurant
The clubhouse restaurant has breathtaking lighthouse and ocean views. Battered fish and chips is the go-to order according to reviewers.
Afternoon
Drive to Shannon (~2h 30m)
